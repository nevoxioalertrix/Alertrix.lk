About AlertRix

AlertRix's vision is to empower seniors to live independently and safely in their homes, supported by cutting-edge AI technology. We are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for seniors and their families through innovative solutions.

Our Story

AlertRix was launched in 2025 by a dynamic team, including both compassionate healthcare experts and skilled IoT & development specialists. We recognized the urgent need for elderly care to move beyond traditional methods and adopt a proactive, personalized approach.
